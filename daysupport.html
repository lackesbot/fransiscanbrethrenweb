St. Michaels Day Support

Individualized services addressing the needs of developmentally disabled participants, promoting independence and personal growth.

About US

St. Michaels Day Support Program (SMDS) is a licensed non-profit day activity program under the auspieces of the Franciscan Brethren of St. Phillip. It is a Medicaid Waiver Developmental Disability Activity Program open Monday through Friday 8am - 3pm. The purpose of the SMDS program is to provide individualized and personal services that address the needs of each developmentally / intellectually disabled participant. Our program is designed to allow the participants to have an opportunity to increase their activities of daily living, increase their independence, social skills, and community awareness opportunities. It is also designed to encourage self-esteem, provide structure, and promote productive efforts that support the personal growth and goals of each individual.


Service Overview

Our Day Support Services provide individualized programming for adults with developmental disabilities, focusing on skill development, community integration, and personal growth. The program operates on weekdays and offers structured activities designed to promote independence and enhance quality of life.

We believe that every individual has the capacity to learn, grow, and contribute to their community. Our person-centered approach ensures that each participant's unique abilities, preferences, and goals are recognized and supported. Our experienced team works closely with participants and their families to develop individualized plans that foster achievement and satisfaction.


Support Activities

St. Michaels Day Support offers a comprehensive range of activities designed to promote various aspects of personal development:

  • Life Skills Training: Daily living skills, money management, and personal care.
  • Community Integration: Outings and activities that promote community participation.
  • Therapeutic Recreation: Engaging activities that promote physical and mental well-being.
  • Communication Enhancement: Support for developing effective communication skills.
  • Social Skills Development: Building relationships and interpersonal skills.
  • Creative Expression: Art, music, and other creative outlets for self-expression.
  • Health and Wellness: Physical fitness and wellness activities.

Benefits

Our Day Support Services provide numerous advantages for participants and their families:

  • Individualized support tailored to personal goals and abilities.
  • Opportunities to develop and practice life skills in a supportive environment.
  • Meaningful community participation and social interaction.
  • Professional guidance from experienced support staff.
  • Relief for family caregivers who need time for work or personal responsibilities.
  • Consistent routine that promotes stability and security.
  • Access to therapeutic activities and interventions.
  • Coordination with healthcare providers and family members.

Eligibility

Individuals eligible for our Day Support Services typically include those with developmental disabilities who would benefit from structured daytime programming. Eligibility is determined based on:

  • Have a documented developmental disability.
  • Are enrolled in the Virginia DD Waiver(Medicaid Developmental Disabilities Waiver) or meeet DBHDS (Virginia Department of Behavioral Health & Developmental Services)licensing criteria.
  • Require 24/7 supervision, skill building, and routine support.
