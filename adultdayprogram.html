St. Clare Adult Day Program

Specialized support for adults with mental health concerns in a community setting, providing structured activities and social engagement.

Program Overview

St. Clare Adult Day Program is designed specifically for adults with mental health concerns who benefit from structured activities, social interaction, and professional support in a community setting. The program provides a safe and nurturing environment where participants can engage in meaningful activities while receiving the care and support they need.


The program operates on weekdays and offers a variety of therapeutic activities tailored to the individual needs and interests of our participants. Our experienced staff members are trained to provide compassionate care while encouraging independence and personal growth.


Daily Activities

Our program offers a diverse range of activities designed to promote physical, mental, and social well-being:

  • Therapeutic Recreation: Engaging activities that promote physical movement and social interaction
  • Creative Arts: Art projects, music therapy, and creative expression opportunities
  • Life Skills Development: Practical skills training for greater independence
  • Coping Skills: Workshops and activities focused on stress management and emotional regulation
  • Health Management: Monitoring and managing health conditions
  • Volunteering: Opportunities to give back to the community
  • Social Groups: Structured group activities that encourage communication and friendship
  • Nutritious Meals: Healthy, well-balanced meals and snacks prepared on-site
  • Medication Management: Professional oversight of medication schedules
  • Health Monitoring: Regular health checks and coordination with healthcare providers

Benefits

Adult Day Program provides numerous benefits for participants and their families:

  • Structured environment that promotes routine and stability.
  • Opportunities for socialization and peer interaction.
  • Professional support from trained staff members.
  • Relief for family caregivers who need time for work or personal responsibilities.
  • Engaging activities that promote mental and physical well-being.
  • Coordination with healthcare providers and family members.
  • Safe and supervised environment from 8am to 4pm.
  • Flexible scheduling options to meet individual needs.

Eligibility Requirements

To participate in our Adult Day Program, individuals must meet the following criteria:

  • Must be a volunteer admission
  • Be 18 years of age or older
  • Currently receiving mental health services
  • Must have a primary mental health Diagnosis.
  • Be able to function in group activities/settings with minimal supervision

Participants must also meet ONE of the criteria below:

  • Have experienced a long time or repeated psychiatric hospitalizations.
  • Poor daily living skills and interpersonal skills.
  • Have a limited or nonexistent support system.
  • Be unable to function in the community without intensive intervention.

Our team conducts thorough assessments to ensure that each participant is appropriately matched with our services. We work closely with families and healthcare providers to develop individualized plans that meet the unique needs of each person.
